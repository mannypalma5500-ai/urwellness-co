Our Story

Built for patients who deserve more than 7 minutes.

Jennifer Fedeli founded The Wellness Co because she saw what most of us already know — the traditional healthcare system isn't built to treat the whole person. Rushed appointments, impersonal care, and a one-size-fits-all approach leave people feeling unheard. She created a cash-pay wellness and optimization clinic where the focus is on you: your goals, your data, your results.

Dawn Philp, FNP, leads the clinical team with 12+ years of hands-on experience, an MSN from Western University, a DEA license, and recognition as an ACCMA trainer. When you're a patient here, your provider knows your name, your history, and what you're working toward — because that's the standard of care you deserve.
