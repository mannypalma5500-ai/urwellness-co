How SoftWave Works

SoftWave is FDA-cleared acoustic wave technology that stimulates your body's natural healing response. The technology activates stem cells, increases blood flow, and reduces inflammation and pain—all without surgery, injections, or downtime.

Sessions are 30 minutes long. We apply the SoftWave handpiece to the affected area, delivering precise acoustic waves that penetrate deep tissue. Most patients feel a gentle tapping sensation. No pain, no recovery time, no limitations afterward.
