What to Expect

IV Administration

Our registered nurses administer your hydration drip in a comfortable, private chair. The IV is placed with precision, and you'll relax for approximately 45 minutes while the therapeutic fluids rehydrate you at a cellular level.

Immediate Rehydration

Normal saline IV therapy delivers fluids directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for immediate absorption and rapid rehydration. Feel the difference within minutes.

Electrolyte Restoration

Your hydration drip includes essential electrolytes—sodium, potassium, magnesium, and calcium—to restore cellular balance and support optimal hydration status.

Recovery & Renewal

Whether you're recovering from a night out, travel dehydration, or intense athletic performance, this drip restores your body's fluid balance and energy levels quickly.
