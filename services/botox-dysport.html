Treatment Areas

Forehead Wrinkles

Smooth horizontal lines across the forehead. Botox relaxes the muscles that create these expression lines, revealing smooth skin.

Frown Lines (The "11s")

Vertical lines between the eyebrows. These deepening lines respond beautifully to Botox. You'll look less tired and more approachable.

Crow's Feet

Fine lines at the outer corners of the eyes. Subtle treatment here delivers natural softening without affecting your smile.

Brow Lift

Strategic Botox placement can lift the brow, creating a more youthful arch and opening the eyes.

Lip Flip

Subtle Botox to the upper lip creates fuller-appearing lips without fillers. A natural enhancement.

Bunny Lines

Fine lines on the sides of the nose when you smile. Botox smooths these subtle expression lines.

Neck Bands

Botox can relax the platysma muscle to reduce the appearance of vertical neck bands. Non-surgical neck rejuvenation.

Combination Treatments

Botox works beautifully with dermal fillers, microneedling, and other treatments for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.

Botox vs. Dysport

What's the Difference?

Both Botox and Dysport are neuromodulators that relax expression muscles. Dysport may spread slightly more, making it ideal for larger areas. Botox offers precise control. Both work beautifully. We'll recommend the best option for your goals.

Results Timeline

Initial results appear in 3-7 days. Peak results occur at 2 weeks. Results last 3-4 months. Most clients maintain with treatments every 3-4 months.

Natural, Not Frozen

Our philosophy: Enhance, don't alter. We use appropriate doses to smooth wrinkles while preserving your natural facial expression. You'll look refreshed, not overdone.

Preventive & Corrective

Botox can prevent lines from deepening (preventive use starting in your 30s) or soften existing wrinkles (corrective use). Either way, the results are beautiful.
