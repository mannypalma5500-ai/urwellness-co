What Is the Vampire Facial?

The Vampire Facial combines microneedling with Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) derived from your own blood. The microneedles create controlled micro-injuries, and the PRP's growth factors stimulate collagen production. The result: deep skin renewal from within.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Power

Your blood contains platelets packed with growth factors and healing compounds. PRP concentrates these powerful substances and delivers them directly into your skin, triggering natural regeneration and collagen synthesis.
